01 — About

I'm a developer who treats the browser as a craft surface — every pixel, transition, and millisecond is a decision, not an accident.

Over the past several years I've worked across startups and agencies, shipping marketing sites, dashboards, and design systems. I care most about the gap between "it works" and "it feels right" — that's where I spend most of my time.

Outside of client work, I write about CSS architecture, contribute small utilities to open source, and obsess over type pairings I'll probably never use twice.
