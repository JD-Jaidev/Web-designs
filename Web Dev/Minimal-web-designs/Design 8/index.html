Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship nationwide?


Yes! We securely package and ship our custom PCs across the country with full insurance to ensure it arrives safely at your doorstep.


What kind of warranty do you provide?


We offer a standard 1-year labor warranty on all builds, plus we pass on all original manufacturer warranties for the individual components (typically 3-10 years depending on the part).


Can I provide my own parts?


Absolutely. If you already have a GPU or storage drive, we can build the rest of the system and integrate your existing components.
